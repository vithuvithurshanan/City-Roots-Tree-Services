Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 19, 2025  |  Last Updated: January 31, 2026

City Roots Tree Services is committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data in compliance with applicable U.S. data protection laws, including the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information including your name, address, email address, telephone number, mobile phone number, and communication details when you contact us, request a quote, or use our services.

SMS Privacy Notice

Collection of Information

We collect your mobile phone number when you opt-in to receive SMS communications from City Roots Tree Services. This may occur through our website contact forms, phone calls, or other direct communication channels.

Use of Information

We use your phone number to send you service updates, appointment reminders, project status notifications, and marketing offers related to our tree care services. Message frequency will vary based on your interactions with us and ongoing service needs.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent your mobile phone information or SMS consent data with any third parties or affiliates for their own marketing purposes. All other categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to or deletion of your personal data, including your mobile number and SMS consent. To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@cityrootstreeservices.com or call 716-274-9576.

SMS Opt-In Information

By providing your mobile phone number through our website forms, service inquiries, or other communication channels, you consent to receive SMS messages from City Roots Tree Services. These messages may include service updates, appointment reminders, estimates, promotions, and customer support communications.

How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used to provide services, respond to inquiries, manage appointments, and communicate with you. We may occasionally send promotional or informational messages related to our tree care services.

Mobile Information Sharing Statement

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All other categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

Cookie and Tracking Practices

We use cookies to improve website functionality and user experience. Cookies do not store sensitive personal information. By continuing to use this website, you consent to our use of cookies in accordance with this policy.

Data Security and Handling

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data where applicable under laws such as CCPA and GDPR. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law.
